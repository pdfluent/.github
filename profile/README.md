# PDFluent

The open-source PDF editor. Your documents, your region, your choice.

A privacy-first desktop PDF editor built with [Tauri](https://tauri.app/), [Pdfium](https://pdfium.googlesource.com/pdfium/) and [LibPDF](https://github.com/nicolo-ribaudo/pdf-lib). Free for individuals and small businesses. Paid tiers for teams and enterprises.

## Why PDFluent?

- **3 MB binary** -- not 7 GB like Adobe Acrobat
- **Works offline** -- no account, no cloud, no telemetry
- **Open source** (AGPL-3.0) -- inspect every line, contribute, fork
- **Data sovereignty** -- you pick where your files live: local, your own cloud, or PDFluent-managed storage in the region of your choice
- **EU company** -- US Cloud Act does not apply
